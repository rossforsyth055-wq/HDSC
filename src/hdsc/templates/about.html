About Us

Our story, mission, and the people behind HDSC

Our Mission

Hearts Disabled Supporters Club exists to bring together supporters of Heart of Midlothian FC who have disabilities, their families, carers, and friends. We aim to create an inclusive community where everyone can share their passion for the Jambos.

What We Do

  • Organise accessible match day experiences at Tynecastle Park
  • Host social events and gatherings for members
  • Advocate for improved accessibility at the stadium
  • Provide a support network for disabled supporters
  • Work closely with Heart of Midlothian FC on inclusion initiatives

Our History

Founded by a group of passionate Hearts supporters who wanted to ensure that disability was never a barrier to supporting the team they love. Since our formation, we have grown into a thriving community of members who share a common bond - our love for Hearts.
