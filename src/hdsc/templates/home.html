Welcome to HDSC

We are a community of Heart of Midlothian supporters dedicated to ensuring that all fans, regardless of disability, can enjoy the beautiful game together. Our club provides support, organises events, and advocates for accessibility at Tynecastle Park and beyond.

Connect with fellow Hearts supporters who understand and share your experiences.

We work with the club to improve accessibility and ensure all fans are welcomed.
